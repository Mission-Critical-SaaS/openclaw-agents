## Overview

Hour Timesheet is a DCAA-compliant cloud-based time and attendance tracking solution designed for government contractors and commercial businesses. The software enables organizations to track employee hours, manage leave, monitor job costs, and ensure compliance with government regulations.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Cloud-based SaaS solution (no servers or network infrastructure needed)
- DCAA compliant for government contractors
- Pricing: $8 per user per month (no hidden fees, no contracts, cancel anytime)
- Free setup and training included
- Integrates with QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, ADP, Paychex, Paylocity, and Insperity

**Does Hour Timesheet Support both QuickBooks Online and Desktop?**
Yes, Hour Timesheet provides simple one-click integration with both QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop.

Yes, Hour Timesheet is a cloud-based SaaS solution. No servers or network infrastructure is required. As long as you have internet access, you have access to Hour Timesheet.

**Does Hour Timesheet contain employee leave management?**
Yes, Hour Timesheet can manage employee leave accruals. Configuration options include weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, and monthly accrual calculations with flexible policy setup.

**What is the minimum monthly license?**
At $8 per user per month, there is no minimum. Pricing starts at $8/user with no additional fees or hidden costs.

**Does Hour Timesheet support mobile time tracking?**
Yes, Hour Timesheet is accessible on any smart phone, tablet, or iPad with a mobile app available for iOS and Android devices.

**What kind of security does Hour Timesheet use to protect our company data?**
Hour Timesheet is PCI compliant and never shares information with third parties. The application uses a 2048-bit SSL certificate for secure communication. All customer websites are SSL secured, and all sensitive data is encrypted and not readable by humans.

**Does Hour Timesheet integrate with 3rd party payroll software solutions?**
Yes, Hour Timesheet will create a simple report that allows you to give your payroll company the files and data they need. Hour Timesheet works with ADP, Paychex, Paylocity, Insperity, and more.

**What features make Hour Timesheet DCAA compliant?**
Hour Timesheet achieves DCAA compliance through: detailed audit trail of all entries and changes, employee electronic signatures/attestation, supervisor approvals, change reason requests, and correction timesheet workflows for government contractor requirements.
